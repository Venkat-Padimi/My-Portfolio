Custom PC Build — Specifications

This project documents a full custom PC build made to balance gaming and development workloads: fast compile times, reasonable GPU performance for ML experimentation, and clean thermals for prolonged use.

What I did

  • Requirements gathering: target uses, budget, noise constraints
  • Part selection & compatibility checks (CPU, MB, RAM, GPU, PSU, Case)
  • Design of airflow and cable management
  • Assembly and debugging
  • Benchmarking (CPU, GPU, SSD), thermals and power draw
  • Final photos and build log

Build Log / Lessons

Document things: torque on cooler screws, BIOS settings, and how I fixed an inconsistent POST due to a loose PCIe power cable. Also learned to prioritize a good PSU and fan curve tuning.

Critical Upgrade: Initially paired the RTX 4060 with an older 450W unit, which caused the older PSU to catch fire and short circuit. Upgraded to a Cooler Master 650W Bronze PSU to ensure sufficient headroom and reliable power delivery, eliminating previous power-related crashes.
